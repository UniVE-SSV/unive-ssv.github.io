**June 4-5, 2026, Venice, Italy**


Following the success of [CSV 22]({% link events/_posts/2022-05-20-csv.md %}), [CSV 23]({% link events/_posts/2023-05-25-csv.md %}), [CSV 24]({% link events/_posts/2024-06-06-csv.md %}), and [CSV 25]({% link events/_posts/2025-06-05-csv.md %}), the Symposium on "Challenges of Software Verification" will take place on Thursday 4th and Friday 5th June 2026 in the [Aula Baratto](https://www.unive.it/pag/30119/) of Ca’ Foscari University of Venice. The 2-day event will be organized in several sessions. The scope of the symposium will cover theoretical results in the field of software verification, their practical applications, novel and innovative tools, and their impact of software verification in software engineering and DevOps practices. The symposium will not have published proceedings, but authors of selected abstracts will be invited to submit a full paper to a special issue of an international scientific journal. The extended version of the talks presented at CSV 2022 has been published in [Springer-Nature](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-981-19-9601-6), the ones of CSV 24 have been published in a [special chapter of STTT](https://link.springer.com/journal/10009/volumes-and-issues/26-4), the ones of CSV 24 have been published in a [special issue of STTT](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10009-024-00778-7), and the articles submitted at CSV 2025 are currently under review for a special issue of STTT.
